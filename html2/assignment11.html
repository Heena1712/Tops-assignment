Tom and Jerry are animated characters, a cat and mouse duo, known for their rivalry and comedic chases in animated shorts. Tom is a bluish-grey and white domestic shorthair cat, while Jerry is a brown house mouse. Their cartoon series, created by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera, featured slapstick comedy and a recurring chase dynamic.
Tom Cat:
Originally named "Jasper," Tom is a domestic shorthair cat, usually portrayed as a more pampered character in the cartoons.
Jerry Mouse:
Initially referred to as "Jinx," Jerry is a brown house mouse, often depicted as the protagonist in his rivalry with Tom.
