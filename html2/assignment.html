Mahatma Mandir gandhinagar

Mahatma Mandir, a convention and exhibition center in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, India, was built by the Government of Gujarat as a tribute to Mahatma Gandhi's life and philosophy. Inaugurated in 2011, it is designed to be environmentally friendly and features a spacious complex for large events, including convention halls, exhibition centers, and the Dandi Kutir museum.
